Ingredients
    4
    large egg yolks
    2/3 cup
    granulated sugar
    1 1/2 cups
    whole milk
    1
    vanilla bean or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    1 1/2 cups
    heavy cream
Directions
    Chill the ice cream bowl
    Prepare an ice bath
    Whisk the yolks and sugar together
    Warm the milk
    Temper the egg-sugar mixture
    Add the tempered egg-sugar mixture
    Cook the ice cream base until thickened
    Strain the ice cream base
    Add the cream
    Chill completely
    Churn the ice cream base
    Freeze the ice cream until solid
